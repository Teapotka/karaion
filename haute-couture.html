THE OUR PRINCIPAL RULE IN HAUTE COUTURE BY KARAION:
"ONE DRESS - ONE CLIENT."
WE NEVER MAKE COPY OF DRESS FOR ANOTHER PERSON. THIS IS THE ONE OF HARDEST AND UNBREAKABLE OUR RULES.
We proudly want to say you that all our Haute Couture ( including prêt-à-porter ) dresses are completely made in our atelier, including embroidery, for which we have a special school (@embroidery__school), where we teach young talents to this absolutely fantastic art of embroidery, based on experience captured from old Italian craftmanships
In the understanding of KARAION, Haute Couture is, firstly, a dialogue between the designer and the client, because it is like a special gift donated to enjoy and make your moments unique and unforgettable.

Based on the dialogue with the client, the designer seeks to create something special, based on the feelings gained during the dialogue, to reveal a unique personality, to complement the charisma and charm of the client, to help you feel like no one else, knowing that your for dress is requires hours and hours painstaking handwork and a thread of soul embedded in each creation.
