WHO WE ARE?
We are a fashion brand based in Ukraine, founded by designer Illia Karaion. We specialize in Haute Couture and pret-a-porter de luxe and luxury goods.
All our Haute Couture and Prêt-à-Porter items is made inside atelier ILLIA KARAION, from the finest quality materials from Italy, by our highly expert team, respecting all traditions of the savoir-faire and highest standards of tailoring . Essential key-words of definition our creations is exceptional quality, craftsmanship and knowledge putted in every single detail, often in not visible parts, and in all what we create. All our products are absolutely unique and of supreme quality.
“ SIMPLE SHIRT NEVER BE A SIMPLE SHIRT, IF IS MADE BY KARAION”.
How we create?
For all piece what we create we put in a lot of energy and passion, craftsmanship to create it at the highest level. Everything is started from bring moment, moment in which the idea is born. Since the idea is captured in the silhouette, I transfer it on the paper, the exact lines and forms of future dress with all annotations. At this level, fabrics, colors, embroidery of the future creation are thought out.
Moulage
Once finished the sketch, Illia Karaion personally with the modelist start to create moulage on the mannequin. Build and rebuild to get the exact shape as intended.
Cutting
Once the moulage is finished, the pieces will be transferred one by one, manually onto the fabric and cut.
Assembling
The assembly phase of all parts to create one special dress. All parts will be assembled, pinned and stitched. In the case of couture products, hand-sewn of course.
